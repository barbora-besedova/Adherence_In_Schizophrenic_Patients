# Adherence in Schizophrenic Patients

This repository contains a bachelor thesis focused on the analysis of adherence in long-term digital monitoring of symptoms in patients with schizophrenia.

The work investigates how patients and carers interact with a digital monitoring system over time, with emphasis on adherence patterns, missing responses, dropout behaviour, and factors that may influence participation in symptom monitoring.

## Project Overview

Digital monitoring systems can support the long-term follow-up of patients with schizophrenia by collecting regular symptom-related information. However, the usefulness of such systems strongly depends on whether patients and carers continue to respond consistently over time.

This thesis explores adherence in the context of long-term symptom monitoring and aims to better understand:

- how adherence changes over time,
- how often participants respond to scheduled questionnaires,
- which behavioural patterns appear in the data,
- how missing responses, dropouts, alerts, and blocked periods affect adherence evaluation,
- whether selected patient-related variables may help explain adherence differences.
